Interesting facts about chameleon

  • It is distinctive and highly specialized clade of Old World lizards
  • Almost 202 species
  • Many species have the ability to change color
  • They are adapted for climbing and visual hunting

Interseting facts about pigeon

  • Columbidae is a bird family consisting of pigeons
  • These are stout-bodied birds with short necks
  • They primarily feed on seeds, fruits, and plants
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet and differentiate between photographs
